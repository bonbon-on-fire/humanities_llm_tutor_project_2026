Sustainable Economic Development — MIT OpenCourseWare

This course explores the application of environmental and economic development planning, policy, and management approaches to urban neighborhood community development. Through an applied service learning approach, students prepare a comprehensive sustainable development plan for Viet-AID — a CDC serving Vietnamese refugees and immigrants in the Fields Corner neighborhood of Dorchester, Boston — testing how sustainable development concepts and environmental and economic planning tools can advance specific community goals within the constraints of a real neighborhood and organization.
