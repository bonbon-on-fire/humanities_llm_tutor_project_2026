Assignment Overview
This week you will build on your Week 6 research by analyzing how your city's climate initiatives connect to transformation pathways and address environmental stressors.

Assignment Task
Complete the Initiatives, Pathways and Stressors Table using your Week 6 findings.

Step 1: List Key Initiatives
Identify the key initiatives and actions being proposed and implemented in your case study city (from your Week 6 research).

Step 2: Connect to Pathways
For each initiative, identify which of the 6 Pathways for Urban Transformation it relates to.

Step 3: Link to Stressors
Identify which environmental stressors each initiative addresses.

Step 4: Discuss
Do not submit your finished table; rather, post about your findings in the Discussion Panel.

The 6 Pathways for Urban Transformation
1. Integrate mitigation and adaptation
2. Coordinate disaster risk reduction and climate change adaptation
3. Co-generate risk information
4. Focus on disadvantaged populations
5. Advance governance, finance and knowledge networks
6. Equity and Environmental Justice
