CITY Case Study Investigation

Recommended due date: May 19 by 10:00am GMT (6:00am EDT). Final due date: August 4 by 10:00am GMT (6:00am EDT).

This assignment is required for Certificate learners, but all learners are encouraged to complete the City Case Study Investigations.

This assignment will be reviewed by the course instructors for content, but no direct instructor feedback will be offered.

Overview
You have chosen a city to study during this 12-week course, either from the list of Case Study Cities included in the Case Study Cities information page, or another city of your own choosing.

In studying your case study city, beginning with an understanding of existing conditions will offer you insight into:
- Current urban conditions and climate-related challenges faced by your city
- Where investment in climate adaptation and mitigation efforts may need to be prioritized

Assignment Tasks
You will complete a Geographic and Demographic Data Table for your chosen city. Gather information on your city's location, population, population race/ethnicity breakdown, physical area, income per capita, GDP, and Köppen climate zone. Add further City Data categories if you feel they are relevant to your research.

Do not submit your Table; rather, share your work by making a post in the Discussion Panel about your findings.
