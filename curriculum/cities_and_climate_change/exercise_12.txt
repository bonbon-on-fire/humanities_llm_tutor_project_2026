City Case Study Investigation — Final Climate Action Plan Prospectus

FINAL due date: August 4 by 10:00am GMT (6:00am EDT).

This assignment is required for Certificate learners, but all learners are encouraged to complete the City Case Study Investigations.

This assignment will be reviewed by course instructors for content, and learners will be expected to submit a self-assessment for grading.

Assignment Overview
This week you will complete your Climate Action Plan Prospectus. Using your research from last week, and what you have learned from fellow learners in the Discussion Panel, you will write final versions of your 2-3 components of a Climate Action Plan, integrating the policy recommendations you have researched, as you discuss how your Climate Action Plan components address the climate and equity challenges identified in your city. Consider the actors that need to be involved in decision-making processes, and how your policy recommendations make space for their contributions.

Assignment Completion Process
You will complete this assignment in the Graded Response, however your grade will be a self-assessment exercise. Follow the instructions in the ORA for how to complete a Self-Assessment.

For your Week 12 submission, you will ONLY WRITE FINAL VERSIONS OF YOUR 2-3 COMPONENTS. You do not need to combine these with other learners' work, and you do not have to submit more than your 2-3 components. Once you have written your final Components, submit them to the Graded Response and complete a Self-Assessment.

Following completion of your Prospectus, use the Discussion Panel to summarize and share the key features of your Prospectus with fellow learners. Use the discussion to reflect on how this Case Study Investigation has helped you understand climate action in cities, and how it might support your future work or studies.

See the examples of a Final Climate Action Plan Prospectus (provided in the course materials) for reference.
