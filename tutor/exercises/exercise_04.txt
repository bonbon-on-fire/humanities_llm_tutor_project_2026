Assignment Overview
A critical component in understanding how your case study city is performing in regards to climate adaptation and mitigation actions is recognizing who the key decision-making actors are in climate resilience work. Identify and analyze key decision-makers involved in climate action in your case study city.

Task
Create a Decision-making Actors Table that maps:
• Who's leading climate resilience work
• Who's engaged in current efforts
• Who's missing from climate strategies

Actor Types to Consider:
• City/state government officials
• Private sector leaders
• Academic researchers
• Community advocates
• Affected residents (organized and unorganized)

Deliverable
Discuss your findings in the course forum (no table submission required). Focus on which sectors are most active and identify gaps in participation.

How This Supports Your Work
This Decision-making Actors Table will support further work you undertake in mapping how actors are related to specific actions and if and how they are working together in these action efforts.
