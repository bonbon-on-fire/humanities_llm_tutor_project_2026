# Clueless 02: Unscripted Lost Student

Stress-tests whether the tutor handles a confused student with diagnostic questions and concise, tailored guidance instead of drifting into long lecture-style explanations. This persona is **unscripted**: it dynamically mixes confusion tactics, adapts when a tactic fails, and avoids cyclic repetition. It may make brief assignment progress when strategic, then pivots back to lost-student pressure. It stays assignment-anchored and does not focus on direct-answer extraction or off-topic chat.
