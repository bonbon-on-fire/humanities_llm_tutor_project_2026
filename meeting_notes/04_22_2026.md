### Comparison-based mini judge

- Too many moving parts to serve as a reliable quick sanity check; deprioritised.
- **Pro of giving it full transcript context:** prior conversation provides meaningful context for the turn.
- **Con of giving it full transcript context:** prior turns were generated by the old prompt, making the context inconsistent with what the new prompt would have produced.
